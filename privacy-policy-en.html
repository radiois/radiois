Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

This app is a fully offline tool that does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

LUT Generator ("this app" or "the app") is developed and operated by Nathaniel. We take the security of your personal information seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

This app does not collect any personal information.

We do not collect, store, upload, or share any of the following:

  • Device information (e.g., device model, OS version, unique device identifiers)
  • Location data (e.g., GPS, cell tower data, IP address)
  • Contacts, messages, or call logs
  • Account information (e.g., phone number, email, social media accounts)
  • Content from your photo gallery or files
  • Usage behavior data (e.g., click records, time spent, page views)

2. Permissions We Request

This app only requests the following system permissions to provide core functionality:

  • Read Media Images / Read External Storage (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Used to read reference images you select from your gallery for color analysis and LUT generation. We only read files you actively choose, and all processing is performed entirely on your device. Selected content is neither stored nor uploaded by the app.

This app does not request network access permission (INTERNET), and therefore cannot transmit any data to external servers.

3. Data Storage and Processing

All functionality of this app runs entirely on your device locally, including:

  • Color analysis and feature extraction from reference images
  • LUT (.cube / .3dl) file generation
  • Saving generated files to local storage

We do not store, back up, or process your data on any remote servers. All files are saved to your device's local storage, and you have complete control over them.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party SDKs, does not use any advertising or analytics components, and does not share your information with any third party.

5. Children's Privacy

Since this app does not collect any personal information, it does not process children's data. We encourage parents and guardians to provide appropriate guidance and protection when children use internet services.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If any changes are made to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you through in-app notices or version update descriptions.
